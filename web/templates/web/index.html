    Frequently Asked Questions

    Got questions? We’ve got answers! We want your experience to be as smooth and enjoyable as possible. Here are some of the most frequently asked questions to help you navigate our platform. If you don’t see what you're looking for, don’t hesitate to reach out to our support team – we're always here to assist you!

    • How do I register a shop on ShopSync?

      You first need to create an account and sign in. To register a shop, simply click on the "Register" button on our homepage, fill in your shop details, and submit the required information. Once your shop is registered, you’ll gain access to the shop dashboard where you can start adding products and manage your store.

    • How do I make a purchase on ShopSync?

      Browse through the available shops and products, select the items you want to buy, add them to your cart, and proceed to checkout. You can choose from multiple secure payment options to complete your purchase.

    • What payment methods do you accept?

      We accept a variety of secure payment methods, including credit/debit cards, mobile money, and bank transfers. All payments are processed through our secure payment gateway.

    • How can I track my order?

      Once your order has been shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number via email or SMS. You can use this number to track the status of your order directly on the courier’s shop.

    • Can I change my order after I’ve placed it?

      Once an order is placed, it is processed immediately for efficiency. However, if you need to make changes, please contact the shop helpdesk through their phone or email as soon as possible. They will assist you if possible.
